    Day 3 - George Lucas Day!

    This day was a mixed blessing. I never thought a Star Wars Celebration would turn into an ultimate test of my mettle.

    This was a rough rough way to start the day. Minimal sleep, got in line at around Midnight and was treated to some of the worst spring weather Indy could've possibly belted out. Less than 40 F, gusting winds, and rain. The kind of cold that cuts you right to the bone.

    It was a huge endurance test to stay in. I did but I shivered uncontrollably for 2-3 hours after I got inside - a sure sign that hypothermia was in process. Not good.

    Thank god my E-1 is weather proof - and I can say that it truly is because it definitely got wet, and cold as it was exposed to the elements.

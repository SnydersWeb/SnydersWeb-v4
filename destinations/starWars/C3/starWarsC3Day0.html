    The Night Before

    People were starting to filter in and check into their hotels. Walking around what parts of the convention center were open, it was obvious people had come from all parts of the world.

    This was Wednesday Evening. The few, the proud, the ones who are gonna need a shower Thursday morning... Celebration III is over 12 hours away yet. That's dedication. I for one value a warm bed and a hot shower!

    I'd be getting in the back of this line soon enough...




    Where can I get one of those?
    (click to enlarge)
    Lot's of captions for this one. Funny checking seeing an Indy Police officer checking out the fan weapons. Though it does make sense considering the original weapons at their cores actually have a REAL weapon. Han's famous blaster pistol is based on a WWII Mauser Broomhandle. The Storm Trooper's guns are based on a Sterling. So it makes sense to check them out to keep someone from bringing in a true weapon.

    Star Wars Celebrations

    Items of Interest:
    • Celebration II
    • Celebration III
    Normally a month before a new Star Wars movie comes out, they put on the Star Wars Celebration. I've come to call them "Lucas Line Festivals" as lines are quite common. In spite of that, they are great fun. You get to meet the stars, hear some of the inside stories behind things, and meet thousands of fellow fans.

    Celebration II has pictures from the Star Wars Celebration II held in Indianapolis, Indiana at the Indy Convention Center in May 2002.

    Celebration III has pictures from the Star Wars Celebration III held in Indianapolis, Indiana at the Indy Convention Center in April 2005.

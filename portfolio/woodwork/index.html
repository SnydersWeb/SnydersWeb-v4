    Since getting married, my wife has encouraged me to branch out and create some crafts with her. Oftentimes I cut things for her Tole painting. Since then I've branched out and work on some other stuff - especially when it helps teach our son things!

    This is a list of some of my creations.

    Hide-Away Vice - After I constructed my garage shelving, I needed a place to put my Dad's vice. This was my solution.

    Paint Cabinet - My wife likes to tole paint so this is the cabinet I created with the help of a former coworker Allen's expertise (the plans were all mine though).

    Computer Stand - After getting my DeWalt portable table saw I created this somewhat ambitious project. A stand for my computer to sit on that doesn't use any fasteners for assembly.

    Desk Organizers - I've made a couple of these, simple desktop organizers made from a single piece of project board.
